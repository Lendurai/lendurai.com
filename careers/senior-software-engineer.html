Senior Software Engineer - Autonomous Systems

About the company and the role

At Lendurai, we enable unmanned aerial vehicles (UAVs) to operate independently of global navigation satellite systems and radio control, using computer vision and machine learning. We partner with multiple UAV manufacturers to deliver cost-effective capabilities to public sector and defence customers. Our team has deep experience in aerospace engineering, defence tech, and autonomous systems, with backgrounds at companies like Bolt (EE), Open Cosmos (UK), and Starship Technologies (EE).

We're looking for a Senior Software Engineer to lead the development of autonomous navigation and control systems for our drone fleet. This role combines deep technical expertise in robotics and autonomous systems with hands-on development of mission-critical software. You'll be at the forefront of creating robust, reliable systems that enable UAVs to operate independently of traditional navigation methods.

What you will be doing

  • Design and implement autonomous navigation and control systems for UAV fleets
  • Develop and optimize algorithms for path planning, obstacle avoidance, and autonomous decision-making
  • Create robust software architectures for real-time drone control systems
  • Lead technical design discussions and code reviews
  • Collaborate with hardware teams to integrate software with drone systems
  • Mentor junior engineers and contribute to team growth
  • Work on cutting-edge computer vision and machine learning solutions

We are looking for

Required experience:

  • 5+ years of experience in software development
  • Strong background in robotics, control systems, or autonomous vehicles
  • Expertise in C++, Python, or similar languages
  • Experience with ROS (Robot Operating System)
  • Knowledge of control theory and navigation algorithms
  • Strong problem-solving and analytical skills
  • Excellent communication and teamwork abilities
  • Fluency in English (additional European languages are a plus)

Preferred backgrounds:

  • Experience in aerospace or defense industry
  • Background in computer vision or machine learning
  • Experience with embedded systems and real-time software
  • Track record of delivering mission-critical systems

What we offer

  • Competitive cash salary and equity options
  • A mission-driven team that values ownership, curiosity, and a hands-on approach to solving defence challenges
  • Opportunity to work on cutting-edge autonomous systems
  • Modern office in Tallinn, Estonia
  • Relocation assistance available
  • Health insurance
  • Professional development opportunities
  • Flexible working hours

Join Lendurai to be part of the forefront of defence technology innovation.
