Head of Business Development

Tallinn, Estonia (Hybrid)

About the company and the role

At Lendurai, we enable unmanned aerial vehicles (UAVs) to operate independently of global navigation satellite systems and radio control, using computer vision and machine learning. We partner with multiple UAV manufacturers to deliver cost-effective capabilities to public sector and defence customers. Our team has deep experience in aerospace engineering, defence tech, and autonomous systems, with backgrounds at companies like Bolt (EE), Open Cosmos (UK), and Starship Technologies (EE).

We're looking for a Head of Business Development to lead our B2G sales, build partnerships with UAV manufacturers, expand into new defence markets, and shape our go-to-market strategy. You'll be responsible for qualifying our products for procurement, building awareness among key defence stakeholders, and closing deals. You'll also develop partnerships with UAV manufacturers and integrators to expand our market reach.

This is a senior leadership role suited for someone who understands both the technical and business aspects of defence technology sales, and can operate credibly with government stakeholders and industry partners.

What you will be doing

  • Lead B2G sales efforts with defence and public sector customers
  • Build partnerships with UAV manufacturers and system integrators
  • Qualify products for procurement in target markets
  • Develop and execute market entry strategies
  • Build relationships with key stakeholders in the defence ecosystem
  • Close strategic deals and ensure successful delivery
  • Shape product roadmap based on market needs

We are looking for

Required experience:

  • 7+ years in B2G sales, business development, or partnerships
  • Track record of closing complex, long-cycle defence deals
  • Experience with defence procurement processes
  • Strong network in European defence industry
  • Excellent communication and negotiation skills
  • Ability to understand and communicate technical concepts

Preferred backgrounds:

  • Business development leads from defence tech companies
  • Sales directors from aerospace or UAV manufacturers
  • Military officers with procurement or industry experience
  • Defence industry consultants with strong networks

What we offer

  • Competitive cash salary and equity options
  • Strategic ownership of Lendurai's market expansion
  • Direct influence on product and company strategy
  • High-impact role in a fast-growing defence tech company
  • Opportunity to shape the future of autonomous systems

Join Lendurai to be part of the forefront of defence technology innovation.
